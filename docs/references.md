# References — Prior Art Case Studies

Titles studied while shaping this demo, and the specific lessons we take from each.

| Title | What we borrow |
|-------|----------------|
| **Puzzling Places** | Reverence for photoscanned/photogrammetry models; tabletop-scale, calming, low-pressure assembly. |
| **Color-A-Tube** | A direct "coloring" interaction and palette handling inside a relaxing frame. |
| **Cubism** | Minimalist, intuitive VR object manipulation; clean, near-diegetic UI. |
| **Jigsaw Night** | Cozy ambiance, gentle pacing, comfortable (often seated) play. |
| **Powerwash Simulator VR** | The deeply satisfying "reveal a clean/colored surface" loop — our closest mechanical cousin. |

## Synthesis

The demo aims to combine **Powerwash's reveal satisfaction** with **Puzzling Places' reverence for
photoscanned spaces**, wrapped in the **cozy, low-stakes tone** of Jigsaw Night and the **clean
manipulation** of Cubism. The result should feel meditative, tactile, and welcoming — never a chore.
